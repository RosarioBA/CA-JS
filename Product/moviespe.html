Hidden By The Moon

"Hidden by the Moon" is an intense space thriller that follows astronaut Alex Walker and her crew as they journey to the moon to investigate a mysterious lunar anomaly. However, the lunar environment proves treacherous, and personal secrets emerge, turning their mission into a battle for survival. With stunning visuals and a gripping storyline, the film explores the human spirit and the quest for discovery in the far reaches of space.
